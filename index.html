Projects

Mind Mapper

I've just launched an idea management website, designed to help users record their future plans and to-do items, ensuring they never lose track of their direction. Built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, it offers a unique approach compared to common to-do apps.

Users can organize their tasks by different objectives on the same page and align them with a single goal. Developing this app required more time due to its complexity, but the result is a powerful tool for managing ideas and tasks effectively.

Dragon Game

I've just created a game for a 3-day mini-game jam using JavaScript. This game was inspired by other 2D canvas games, but we approached it with a different concept for controls and positioning. It's actually quite engaging and entertaining, as it captures the attention of the human brain. People seem to love playing it, as it offers a balance of simplicity and challenge.
